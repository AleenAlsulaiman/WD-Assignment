What is The Application Goal?

JedActivities application is an application that provides diverse activities in Jeddah, allowing individuals to engage in various sports and hobbies. All activities are group-oriented to enhance effective community interaction, understanding of others' traditions, and contribute to reducing stress, tension, and obesity in the community. The reward points feature is designed to encourage users to remain loyal to the platform and continue booking activities. It leverages the psychological satisfaction of earning rewards to build a stronger connection between the user and the services provided in our application.
